BRIEF BIOGRAPHY

Dr. Hui Yang has more than 7 years of experince in HCI Research, Data Analytics and User Centered Design of Intelligent and IT products in USA, Sweden, China. He currently works on product design and development at Saltside Technologies - the creator of leading online marketplaces: Bikroy, ikman, Tonaton, and Efritin.

Before joining Saltside, he was a Research Assistant in Laboratory for Intelligent Systems and Analytics (LISA) at Pennsylvania State University while doing his PhD in Industrial Engineering. His doctoral dissertation is User Centered Design of Visual Analytics and Its Applications in Healthcare. Hui has previous R&D experience from Intelligent Automation, Inc. for US federal customers including Department of Defense (DoD), National Institutes of Health (NIH) and National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST).

He holds Master's degree in Human Computer Interaction from the Uppsala University, Sweden, and a M.S.and B.S. in engineering, with a concentration in Instrument Science and Technology, from Harbin Institute of Technology,one of the C9 League in China. He is a member of the Human Factors and Ergonomics Society (HFES), and serves as an officer of the HFES Penn State Chapter. He plays guitar, and is a huge fan of classical music, and award-winning photographer.
